About Island Integration

We design, install, and support luxury smart home systems across the Bahamas — from lighting control and home cinema to enterprise-grade networking and smart security.

Connect with us Call +1 (242) 820-9013

Our Story

Island Integration began with a simple idea: technology should disappear into beautiful spaces. Over the last decade we’ve helped homeowners, developers, and hospitality teams create environments that feel effortless — reliable networks, intuitive control, and cinematic AV without visual clutter.

From Lyford Cay and Old Fort Bay to Ocean Club Estates and Albany, we deliver systems tailored to each property, with craftsmanship that lasts.

What We Believe

  • Design first: Technology should enhance architecture, not fight it.
  • Reliability matters: Enterprise-grade networks are the backbone of every system.
  • One-touch living: Scenes simplify complex spaces into simple experiences.
  • Long-term support: We maintain, monitor, and evolve systems as needs change.
