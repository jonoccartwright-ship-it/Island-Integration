About Island Integration

Bahamas-based Control4 integrator delivering reliable smart home systems for luxury residences and commercial properties.

Our Story

For over a decade, we’ve helped clients across Nassau, New Providence, and the outer islands design and install elegant, dependable technology—so their homes and spaces feel effortless every day. From lighting and climate to audio, cinema, networking, and security, we deliver systems that are as simple as they are powerful.

We are Control4 specialists, with a focus on clean design, meticulous installation, and responsive support. Our work spans luxury residences, boutique hospitality, and commercial developments—always tailored to the property and the people who live and work there.
